Dr.A.P.J.Abdul Kalam

Ex-President/Scientist

Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam was an Indian aerospace scientist who served as the 11th President of India from 2002 to 2007. He was born and raised in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu and studied physics and aerospace engineering.

M.Visvesvaraya

Civil engineer/Statesman

Sir M. Visvesvaraya KCIE FASc, more commonly known as Sir MV, was an Indian civil engineer, statesman and the 19th Diwan of Mysore, serving from 1912 to 1919. He pursued his degree from one of the best and the 3rd oldest engineering college in Asia, College of Engineering, Pune.

Kailash Satyarthi

Social reformer

Kailash Satyarthi is an Indian social reformer who campaigned against child labour in India and advocated the universal right to education
